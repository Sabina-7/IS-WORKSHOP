About BBIS

The Bachelor in Business Information System (BB15) program at Kathmandu University combines the knowledge of business management and information technology to create professional who can bridge the gap betwem business and IT.

About IS Club

The Information Systems Club is a student-led club under BBIS that promotes learning and innovation in the fields of technology, data, business systems, and event organization. The club regularly hosts workshops, seminars, hackathons, and industrial visits

Our Activities

  • Workshops on Programming, Design, and Finance Tools
  • Seminars on Emerging Technologies
  • Grand Lectures from Industry Experts
  • Hackathons and Coding Competitions
  • Industry Visits and Consultations
